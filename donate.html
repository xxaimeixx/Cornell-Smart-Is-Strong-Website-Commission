Let's Make a Difference!

The Smart is Strong Foundation fundraises money for events and initiatives to educate our community, empower tomorrow's leaders, advocate for gender inequality, and mentor students to achieve their dreams. At the end of the academic year, The Smart is Strong Foundation awards college scholarships to local high school students.
