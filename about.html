About

Mission

The Smart is Strong Foundation (SiS) aims to reduce gender inequality in society by empowering young women to reach their highest educational and professional potential.

Source

Educate

The Smart is Strong Foundation educates and persists in learning more about social issues affecting our local community.

Empower

The Smart is Strong Foundation empowers young women to pursue their goals, despite the odds against them.

Advocate

The Smart is Strong Foundation advocates for gender equality, meaning equal opportunities for all in education and the workforce.

Mentor

The Smart is Strong Foundation mentors local high school students, encouraging them to pursue higher education.

We achieve our goals by...

Organizing an annual International Women's Day Conference

Providing college scholarships for high school women

Offering college application assistance to local high school students
